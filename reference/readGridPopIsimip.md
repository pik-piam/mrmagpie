# readGridPopIsimip

Reads in past and future (SSP1-5) gridded population data, from ISIMIP
database, Past data is based on HYDE3.2, while future SSPs are based on
projections from Jones & O'Neill 2016

## Usage

``` r
readGridPopIsimip(subtype)
```

## Arguments

- subtype:

  past (1965-2005) or future (2010-2100)

## Value

A MAgPIE object, cellular 0.5deg resolution, of population (millions)
